Grant Fit Scout

Community College Grants Finder

A Grants.gov shortlist workflow for community colleges, technical education teams, workforce programs, and institutional grant offices.

Grant Fit Scout scores federal opportunities by deadline urgency, applicant fit clues, award details, proposal workload, contact details, and source links. It is built to reduce the first-pass reading burden before a grant team commits time to a full NOFO review.

Example searches:
  • community college workforce development
  • technical education training grants
  • STEM workforce grants
  • cybersecurity education grants

What the report gives you

  • ranked opportunity shortlist
  • days-to-close and urgency labels
  • eligibility notes when available
  • award ceiling and floor clues when available
  • direct Grants.gov links for human review
