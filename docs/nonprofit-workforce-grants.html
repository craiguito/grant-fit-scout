Grant Fit Scout

Nonprofit Workforce Grants

A Grants.gov shortlist for nonprofits looking for workforce development, training, education, and technical-assistance funding opportunities.

Grant Fit Scout helps nonprofit teams decide which federal opportunities deserve a human NOFO review. It scores each opportunity and shows deadline urgency, workload, eligibility notes, award clues, contact information, and direct source links.
